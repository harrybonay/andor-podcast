The Premise

AND / OR is an inside look at the hard choices behind great products.

An AND / OR is the tension between two things you want — knowing you can't fully have both. A decision with consequences that every founder faces.

Speed or quality.
Focus or flexibility.
Legibility or power.

Most teams talk around these choices. This show sits inside them.

Not tactics. Not advice. Decisions, constraints, and what gets lost.
